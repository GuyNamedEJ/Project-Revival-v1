Our Mission

Repent America Now! is an inter-faith, cross-cultural call-to-action for repentance and reconciliation envisioned by the New Birth Institute (NBI). NBI is a faith-based think tank leading in the research, practice and motivation toward Spiritual Growth, Human Empowerment, Public Health and Societal Development. This action is not a centralized initiative but rather a non- hierarchal, decentralized and democratized move of the Holy Spirit rooted in the revelation of the coming Messianic age and informed by the spreading of the gospel of the Kingdom of God to ALL people, in ALL nations. As such, we are not affiliated with a particular religion, denomination, sect or creed.

Our Beliefs

We believe that our environment is of paramount importance to our specific social location or cultural differences. We believe the time has come to cultivate the great command to love throughout the land and create an atmosphere of repentance and reconciliation that culminates with a national proclamation and observance. We believe that Almighty God has chosen our great nation to acknowledge her trespasses and transgressions while utilizing her great wealth, influence and standing to serve as a beacon of light as the entire world is being judged in this same regard.

Indeed, the cataclysmic nature of geopolitical events, climate of racial hate and intolerance, natural disasters, epidemics, extreme poverty and ongoing humanitarian crises all indicate the signs of the time for humility and healing has come. We believe there is great solace and comfort in knowing there is past precedent for such a call and redemptive event. President Abraham Lincoln responded to a similar move of the Holy Spirit in the midst of turbulent times in 1863 to heal and unite our land against the spirit of division by issuing a proclamation for “A Day of National Humiliation, Fasting, and Prayer in the United States of America.” We believe America finds herself once again, in a dark place, in 2022. We believe the time has come for history to repeat itself.

Thank you for your support!

The Petition

Saying, the time is fulfilled. The Kingdom of God is upon us. Turning our hearts to God. Believing the gospel of truth as expressed in all of the Holy Scriptures. And whereas, it is the duty of nations as well as of men, and women, and children to own their dependence upon the sovereign power of God, to confess of their past, present and future sins and transgressions with humble sorrow yet the assured hope that genuine repentance leads to everlasting mercy, grace and eternal reconciliation into the Royal family and Kingdom of God, and to recognize the universal truth, as witnessed and proven throughout history: that those nations only are blessed whose God is Lord, whose aim is love and whose ambitions are guided by the collective aspirations toward realizing the perfect bond of peace and unity. And whereas, this National Call for Repentance of the New Birth Institute is an open invitation and inclusive movement toward repentance and reconciliation for our nation, our communities and our world, culminating with a national proclamation and observance shared by all people, across all faiths and all cultures to heal our great land, I pledge my prayers and support to this cause.
